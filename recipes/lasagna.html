Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for feeding a crowd. This recipe makes a large pan of lasagna, so it's great for serving at family gatherings or potlucks. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 32 ounces cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • ½ cup water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook and stir ground beef until brown. Add spaghetti sauce and simmer 15 minutes.
  3. In a large bowl, combine cottage cheese, 2 cups mozzarella cheese, eggs, parmesan cheese, parsley, salt and pepper.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer noodles, sauce, and cheese mixture. Repeat layers, and top with remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 60 minutes. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
